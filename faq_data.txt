QuickLoan NBFC - Customer FAQ Knowledge Base

COMPANY OVERVIEW

QuickLoan NBFC is a digital lending and financial services company providing personal loans, business loans, education loans, and MSME financing solutions across India. Customers can apply online and receive fast approval decisions.

---

GENERAL QUESTIONS

1. What services does QuickLoan NBFC provide?
   QuickLoan NBFC provides personal loans, business loans, MSME financing, education loans, and loan refinancing services.

2. Is QuickLoan NBFC RBI registered?
   Yes, QuickLoan NBFC operates under RBI guidelines applicable to non-banking financial companies.

3. Who can apply for a loan?
   Salaried individuals, self-employed professionals, small business owners, freelancers, and MSMEs can apply subject to eligibility criteria.

4. Can I apply online?
   Yes, all applications can be completed online through the website or mobile platform.

5. What cities does QuickLoan operate in?
   QuickLoan provides services across major Indian cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, and Ahmedabad.

---

PERSONAL LOAN QUESTIONS

6. What is the minimum personal loan amount?
   The minimum personal loan amount is ₹25,000.

7. What is the maximum personal loan amount?
   Eligible applicants can receive up to ₹15 lakhs depending on income and credit profile.

8. What is the repayment tenure?
   Loan repayment tenure ranges from 6 months to 60 months.

9. What are the interest rates?
   Interest rates start from 11.5% per annum and may vary depending on credit score, income stability, and repayment history.

10. How long does approval take?
    Most loan approvals are completed within 24 to 48 hours after document verification.

11. Are there any hidden charges?
    No hidden charges are applied. All applicable fees are disclosed before loan disbursement.

12. Is collateral required?
    Personal loans are unsecured and usually do not require collateral.

BUSINESS LOAN QUESTIONS

13. What types of businesses are eligible?
    Retail businesses, manufacturers, service providers, startups, and MSMEs may apply.

14. What is the minimum business vintage required?
    Businesses should generally have at least 1 year of operational history.

15. Can startups apply for funding?
    Early-stage startups may apply, but approval depends on revenue consistency and financial documentation.

16. What documents are required for business loans?
    Applicants may need GST returns, bank statements, business registration proof, PAN card, and income documents.

17. What is the maximum business loan amount?
    Business loans can go up to ₹50 lakhs for eligible businesses.

CREDIT SCORE & ELIGIBILITY

22. What is the minimum credit score required?
    A credit score above 700 is generally preferred.

23. Can I apply with a low credit score?
    Yes, applications with lower scores may still be reviewed based on income and repayment capacity.

24. Will applying affect my credit score?
    Loan applications may result in a soft or hard inquiry depending on the verification process.

25. How can I improve my eligibility?
    Maintaining stable income, reducing existing debt, and paying EMIs on time can improve eligibility.
